Setting Up the CSM/FSM Configurable Workspace

Published on [Date] by [Author Name]

The CSM/FSM Configurable Workspace, which stands for Customer Service Management / Field Service Management workspace, is a ServiceNow platform-provided pre-built workspace. It is built primarily for customer service and field service management activities.

Configuring List Categories in the CSM/FSM Configurable Workspace

  1. Log in to ServiceNow as follows:

    1. Use your unique login credentials to access your ServiceNow instance.

  2. Navigate to the Configurable Workspace:

    1. Use the navigation menu's search functionality to discover alternatives relating to "Workspace" or "Configurable Workspace," and then select the appropriate option.
    2. To be more specific, type "sys_ux_list_menu_config.LIST" in the left-hand navigation bar.

  3. Click on CSM/FSM Configurable Workspace List Menu Config:

    The application scope will be in csm/fsm workspace foundation.

  4. The list of categories can be seen in the image.

  5. If you want to edit the category case, you can do it by clicking on "case".

  6. The list of child categories will be seen in the image below.

    When you click on a child category, a new page opens in which you can adjust the conditions as needed.

  7. If you want to provide users with the ability to filter or search for specific categories, you can add filter options to the list component.

  8. Configure the layout of the list component, including the columns you want to display, sorting options, and any additional customization.

  9. Once you're satisfied with the configuration, save and publish your workspace.

  10. After publishing, assign the workspace to specific users or roles so they can access the list of categories.

    You can see all the list categories and child list categories on the workspace.
